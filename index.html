The Gentle Hand of Atta

Baqa'a Valley, West Bank

Film

A feature film by Bruno Sorrentino & Uri Fruchtmann.

For 25 years, Atta Jaber has tended his land in the Baqa'a Valley of the occupied West Bank. Settlers advance. Homes are demolished. Ancient terraces are erased. Atta does not raise his hand in violence. This is his story.
